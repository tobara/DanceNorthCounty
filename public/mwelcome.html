Dance North County is a Premier Dance Center with over 20 Independent Dance Schools and 40 Independent Expert Dance Teachers to fill all your dance needs. Dance North County Ballroom Latin Dance Studio, in north San Diego, California, CA is conveniently located at 535 Encinitas Blvd. near I-5, to serve Encinitas, Carlsbad, Oceanside, Rancho Santa Fe, Del Mar, Carmel Valley, Leucadia, San Marcos, Vista, La Jolla, Escondido and Cardiff by the sea, Solana Beach, La Costa.

International Style - Standard: Waltz, Foxtrot, Tango, Viennese Waltz.
Latin Dances
American Style - Rhythm: Cha Cha, Rumba, Swing, Bolero, Mambo.
International Style - Latin: Cha Cha, Rumba, Paso Doble, Jive, Samba.

Our Ballroom Latin Dance Studio provides Couple Dance Instruction, Dance Lessons and Dance Classes in Ballroom and Latin Dancing. 3 dance studios within our main dance studio provide abudant space for private dance lessons & group dance classes.
